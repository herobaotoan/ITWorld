3. Project ideas:

Application name: RecipeBook. - Overview: RecipeBook will be a mobile phone application or a website, which users can create their own accounts then post their cooking recipes on the account’s walls. Morever, users can like or dislike, save recipe and follow other users to get their latests cooking recipes.
- Motivation: The project will be problably useful for people who like to cook new dishes around the world. Like my mom, she sometimes looks up for some new cooking recipes on some random websites, but she always asks me how to save or share them to our neighbors and relatives. So I came up with this project, RecipeBook is meant to be easy to use for everyone, for all ages. It helps people to save recipes they fancy trying to cook or easily post their own recipes easily with some extra app’s functions.
- Description: Account: every user has to make an account in order to post, save or like the posted recipes. However, everyone can still see posted recipes without log-in. The sign-up and log-in procedure will only ask for emails and passwords, this app is not a market or a trading place so no credit card will be allowed to enter. Profile: the account profile will include some features such as “cooking styles”, “cooking level”. These features will help other users to look for recipes that fits themselves the most.
Posting: After logging into an account, user can post their cooking recipes. The posting procedure will include some features like “Name”, “Ingredients” (Users can add many ingredients they want), “Units”, “Steps” (The more steps the easier) and “More details” (tips, how hard to cook this recipe, any possible problem?). Morever, each of the features can be added with pictures or videos depend on what users want to share.
Interaction between users: Users can follow others if they think that user posted some really good recipes so when that followed user post another, notification will come up. Save recipe is the most important feature, users can save any recipe they want to try then open it later. However, if the posted recipe is deleted, saved recipe will also be deleted. Moreoever, there is like and dislike function, this helps users to determine whether that recipe is good or not. Users can also report inappropriate posts, by doing this the reported posts will be sent to the censorship and hided on the wall.
Policy and report: If any post is reported as inappropriated, there should be a team who look at these and decide to delete or keep the post. Posts must be about food, and the food must not represent sexual content or related to politics. The differences between RecipeBook and other social media platforms: There is no inbox or comment function, so users can chat or leave any comment on other users’s posts.
- Tools and Technologies: The website will need a database to store all of the user’s information and posts. So I think MySQL or any database infrastruture will be needed. The website and the application can be programmed by any language that is appropriated, or maybe like Facebook, which is created by using PHP programming language.
- Skill Required:
+ Coding: PHP or C++ to program the software.
+ Know how to work with MySQL or any other database.
+ Understand the concept and know what experience the project wants to bring to users.
- Outcome: If the project is succesful, everyone who has passion about cooking will have a lovely application to explore new recipes everyday or have a place to share their own ideas. The bad side is that my mom will never need me to help with the IT problem while looking for cooking recipes. Also many jobs will be provided.

5. IT Technologies:
- Cloud Computing
This technology is said to be currently at its infancy [1]. However, its benefit has already been a big deal to the IT world, it deliveries servers, database, intelligence, software and much more through the internet. Its potential is endless, my prediction for the future is this technology will become cheaper as it is still expensive right now, its performance will be faster, reducing network latency, giving companies a lot of flexibility.
Computing used to be all about boxes containing metal and silicon, every single IT workplace always contained those boxes, they consumed much electricity, regularly required updates and they even crashed sometimes [2]. Then cloud computing arrived made a huge impact on every business, it changed everything, all the data are now “floating” and stored remotely, reducing costs of electrical bill and regular system updates, all they need to do is paying monthly subcription.
As for me, besides the video games data, my study works are currently not so much that require a lot of storage space. However, maybe 1 or 2 years from now, I will have to store everything on the cloud.
- Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning:
When talking about the state of the art of AI then it must be about it’s Deep Learning. It is the models that work with non-texual data like image or voice recognition. Those features allow AI the feature that recognize objects, give it the ability to assist in many different fields. Such as in biology, AI helps biologists determine the order of DNA, distinguish proteins and their biological roles [3]. Morever, Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning, the model that focuses on setting AI up the ability to perform works without human interaction. Machine Learning’s goal is to make a computer that can make decisions and predictions even without being programmed. In medicine, the model helps making decions about treatments, medications and what patients need [4].
The potential is endless.
